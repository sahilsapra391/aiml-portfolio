Professional Bio

Who I am

I'm Sahil Sapra — a Technical Product Manager and builder based in Charlotte, North Carolina. I'm the co-founder and Head of Product at SpecHawk, an AI-powered QA automation platform, and previously led product and business analysis work on enterprise aviation software at CAMP Systems. I'm currently pursuing an M.S. in Computer Systems with an AI specialization at Indiana Wesleyan University.

I got into product because I like the seam where user problems meet technical possibility — and AI has made that seam the most interesting place in software. My aspiration is straightforward: keep building AI products that hold up in the real world, and lead teams that do the same. This portfolio documents that journey through my graduate program — the experiments, the builds, the tests, and what each one taught me.

Personal Value Proposition

The value I bring

As a technical product manager who ships products and builds with AI hands-on, I help organizations turn ambiguous AI opportunities into working, tested products. I pair classical product discipline — user research, prioritization, measurable outcomes — with practical AI fluency, so the teams I work with get someone who can interview a customer in the morning and prototype the solution with an LLM that afternoon.
